## 1. Executive Summary

**ProviderSync** is a specialist Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) company that takes the operational and technology burden off Australian healthcare and community-service providers. We combine two capabilities that providers normally have to source from separate vendors:

1. **Healthcare Administration & Operations** — NDIS onboarding, compliance, rostering, payroll and coordination.
2. **IT & Digital Solutions** — websites, hosting, email, branding, SEO and ongoing IT support.

By delivering both **under one roof**, ProviderSync lets small-to-mid-size providers run a professional backend without hiring separate admin staff, IT vendors and marketing agencies — so they can focus on delivering care.

**The one-line pitch:** *We keep everything running in the background so providers stay in sync with their goals, their compliance requirements, and their clients.*

---

## 2. The Problem

Small and growing NDIS, Aged Care and ECE providers face a common squeeze:

- **Compliance is heavy and unforgiving** — NDIS Practice Standards, aged care requirements and incident reporting demand constant admin.
- **Backend work eats clinical time** — rostering, payroll, timesheets, intake and invoicing pull owners away from care.
- **Fragmented vendors** — one company for admin, another for IT, another for marketing. Nothing is joined up.
- **Hiring is expensive** — a full-time admin officer + IT contractor is out of reach for a growing provider.
- **Generic BPOs don't understand healthcare** — they don't know NDIS, SDA/SIL, or aged-care rules.

## 3. Our Solution

A single, personalised backend partner that understands the Australian healthcare compliance environment and delivers **Admin + IT together**, remotely, on affordable packages sized for growing businesses.

### C. Early Childhood Education (ECE)
- Early childhood education centres

## 16. Brand Identity — Logo & Colour Palette

### Colour Palette

Anchored on the healthcare-green + optimistic-amber direction from the existing business document, refined into a usable system.

| Role | Colour | Hex | Use |
|------|--------|-----|-----|
| **Primary — Forest Green** | 🟢 | `#178A4C` | Trust, health, growth. Logo, headers, primary buttons. |
| **Deep Green (dark)** | 🟩 | `#0F5C33` | Footer, dark sections, hover states. |
| **Accent — Amber/Gold** | 🟠 | `#F5A623` | Energy & optimism. CTAs, highlights, the "Sync" mark. |
| **Ink** | ⚫ | `#1A2B23` | Body text, headings. |
| **Mist (light bg)** | ⬜ | `#F4F9F6` | Section backgrounds. |
| **White** | ⚪ | `#FFFFFF` | Base / cards. |

**Why this palette:** green signals health, care and reliability (standard in the sector) while amber differentiates ProviderSync from the sea of blue/green medical brands and conveys warmth, energy and momentum — reinforcing "Sync" and "streamline."

**Accessibility:** Forest Green `#178A4C` and Ink on white both exceed WCAG AA. Use Ink (not white) text on amber for contrast.

### Logo Concept

- **Style:** Clean modern wordmark — **Provider** in Ink/Deep Green + **Sync** in Forest Green, with a small **sync/refresh icon** (two curved arrows forming a circle, or an "S") in amber replacing or sitting beside the "S" of Sync.
- **Symbol meaning:** the two rotating arrows = synchronisation, continuity, "always running in the background."
- **Standalone mark:** the circular sync arrows in a green-to-amber gradient — works as a favicon, social avatar and app icon.
- **Typography:** Poppins (SemiBold for the wordmark, Regular for taglines) — modern, friendly, professional.
- **Clear space & variants:** full-colour, all-white (for dark backgrounds), single-colour green, and mono-black.

**Tagline lockup:** *ProviderSync — Sync Your Backend. Streamline Care.*
